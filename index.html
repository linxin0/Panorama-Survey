Overview

Panoramic vision enables full 360¬∞ perception for VR, robotics, and autonomous driving. This survey systematically reviews panoramic imaging, projection models, and adaptation from perspective to panorama. We summarize 20+ tasks, 300+ papers, and discuss emerging trends like diffusion, 3D Gaussian Splatting, multimodal fusion, and world model.

Panoramic Tasks

Super-resolution, Denoising, Deblurring, Reflection Removal, Visual Quality Assessment

Segmentation, Mapping, Detection, Tracking, Pose Estimation, Saliency Prediction, Layout Detection, Optical Flow Estimation, Keypoint Matching, Lighting Estimation, Depth Estimation

Completion, Novel View Synthesis, Text-guided Generation

Fusion with Text, Audio, and LiDAR
